Muhammad Ali Jinnah

The founder of Pakistan

Here's a time line of Muhammad Ali Jinnah:

  • 1876 - Born in Karachi, Pakistan
  • 1892 - Arrange marriage to Emi Bai
  • 1894 - Wife Emi Bai died and mother passed away few months later
  • 1895 - Became Barrister at age of 18
  • 1900 - Elected as magistrate
  • 1902 - Father passed away
  • 1905 - Started working for the Dada Naoraji
  • 1906 - An All India Muslim League was established
  • 1929 - 2nd wife Ratin Bai dies
  • 1940 - the first talk of parition Started
  • 1947 - Partition of India and formation of Pakistan
  • 1948 - Jinnah died due to Tuberculosis
